Press Here to Start

  • Once you start the game, one of the four colours will be highlighted, along with a corresponding sound, by the system.
  • You will have to press the same colour to proceed.
  • With every level, a new colour is highlighted, in a random sequence.
  • You'll have to remember the sequence and press the colours.
  • For e.g, if red is highlighted in level 1, press red. If green is highlighted in level 2, the sequence is red green. If level 3 highlights red again, the sequence is red green red.
  • If you miss a member of the sequence and press the wrong colour, the game will restart.
  • Good luck and have fun!
